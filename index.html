Welcome

Chorley & South Ribble Model Society is a group of enthusiasts dedicated to the enjoyment of all form of Radio control aircraft.

We welcome all pilots of fixed wing, rotary wing, glider, and electric models to our Flying Club.

We have approximately 20 members of all ages and interests, the club being affiliated to the national body BMFA. The Club field is situated on the outskirts of Chorley in the Yarrow Valley Country Park, and most types of models can be accommodated.

All types of model aircraft are welcome and there is no limit on engine size or model size.

However, it is a condition of our permission to fly that stay within the noise limits defined by both the council and the BMFA.

To take a quick look at where we fly, what we fly, and who flies, feel free to browse our website and be sure to check out our gallery.

We welcome enquiries from people wishing to join whether they are experienced flyer's or new to the hobby.
